! Note about loopbacks: on an ABR, these get placed with the lowest area number the ABR is a part of.

router ospf 1
area 1 nssa
network 10.0.1.0 0.0.0.255 area 1
network 10.1.2.0 0.0.0.255 area 1
network 172.16.0.3 0.0.0.0 area 1
network 172.16.0.6 0.0.0.0 area 1
network 172.16.0.8 0.0.0.0 area 1
network 172.16.0.9 0.0.0.0 area 1
exit

ipv6 router ospf 1
exit
